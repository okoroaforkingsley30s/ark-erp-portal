ARK ONE 1.0.16 — BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT END-TO-END REPAIR
========================================================

This is a general system repair. It does not repair only one lead.
Existing leads, clients, complaints and tickets are preserved.

WORKFLOW INSTALLED
------------------
1. A Business Development Officer captures and owns a lead.
2. Calls, emails, meetings, visits, outcomes and next actions are logged.
3. The lead moves through New, Contacted, Qualified, Proposal and Negotiation.
4. Selecting Won submits the opportunity to the Head of Business Development.
5. The Head approves or rejects it. Rejection returns it to Negotiation with a reason.
6. Approval creates/updates the client and creates the required department handoffs:
   - Operations: service activation and delivery ownership
   - Helpdesk: support profile and service contacts
   - Finance & Accounts: commercial, billing and account setup
   - Inventory: product availability and fulfilment
   - Procurement: supplier fulfilment for unavailable products
   - IT: technical integration and implementation
7. Each receiving department acknowledges, completes or rejects its own handoff.
8. Business Development sees the onboarding progress and receives the response.
9. Business Development records a client complaint and submits it to Helpdesk.
10. Only Helpdesk creates, assigns, manages and finally closes the support ticket.
11. When Helpdesk closes the ticket, CRM receives it for client follow-up.
12. Business Development records a 1–5 satisfaction rating and closes only the CRM complaint record.
    This never reopens, edits or closes the Helpdesk ticket.

NOTIFICATIONS
-------------
- Routine pipeline updates remain in-app.
- Major events use in-app notification plus the configured email outbox:
  Won approval submission, approval/rejection, departmental handoff,
  complaint routing, ticket resolution and rejected/complete handoffs.
- Notification links point only to routes the receiving role can open.

INSTALL AND VERIFY LOCALLY
--------------------------
Run from the repository root:

  npm run validate:migrations
  npm run lint
  npm run typecheck
  npm test
  npx supabase migration up --local

PRODUCTION SAFETY
-----------------
Create and verify a production logical backup first. Then use:

  npx supabase db push --linked --dry-run

The dry run must list only:

  202607220007_business_development_end_to_end_workflow.sql

After review and an approved PR, apply it with:

  npx supabase db push --linked

Do not paste production keys, database passwords or Gmail secrets into chat,
source files, Git commits or screenshots.

ACCEPTANCE TEST
---------------
Use test accounts for Business Developer, Head BD, Operations, Helpdesk,
Finance, Inventory, Procurement and IT. Complete one service lead, one product
lead and one client complaint from creation to closure. Confirm every receiving
role sees only its queue and that major email notifications are queued.
